Week Two

Beginning of my intership journey

TUESDAY

(2.1.2024)

Went to school on the 1st day of practicum to report duty. (gambar ke sekolah pagi2)

Received a personal timetable according to PDPC session. (gambar jadual waktu)

At 8am we had a formal assembly as it is the first day of school in January 2024. (gambar perhimpunan)

At 10 am I have English classes with Form 3 Intelek (gambar kelas 3 Intelek)

WEDNESDAY

(3.1.2024)

At 7.30am I have a relief class at 4 Integriti to start and monitor their examination of PJK papers. (gambar 4 Integ)

During the 4th period, I have English classes with 1 Integriti and they need to submit their exercises to me. (gambar handouts)

Around 11.20am, I got to relief 4 Inovatif class again monitoring them answering examinations.

THURSDAY

(4.1.2024)

During the 5th period, I have relief class with 2 Inspirasi and 2 Ihsan at their school foryer.

At 12pm I need to monitor 4 Intelek's student answering their Science examinations.

FRIDAY

(5.1.2024)

We have Yassiin recital at 7.30 am and I was chosen to join a Fast-Track & Teach Smart programme conducted by SMK Taman Maluri. (gambar poster)

We are in 3 Inspirasi class that was being taught by Sir Amiruddin. (video class)

Around 11.10am, I also need to monitor Form 4 Inspirasi students answering their Chemistry papers.

SWOT ANALYSIS

STRENGTH

Teacher-student Interaction:

Throughout the first week of internship here, I noticed that students of my class have no problem in communicating with me. They are allowed to voice out their ideas or pov in formal or informal way or context (feel free to have a conversation with me too, not strictly about learning only). I also managed to solve a fight among my students.

WEAKNESS

Lack of class control:

The first class that I need to teach in my first week, I could not control their voice loudness during the one to one ice breaking session.

OPPORTUNITIES

Teaching Materials:

It is easier for me to straightaway plan a lesson and do revision with my students since other English teachers provide me the books that I can use. Not to mention it's so easy to deal with the teachers there.

THREATS

Not alert with time to time situation:

I should have been more alert with my students doings and notice from the very start (the source of their fights). I also need to buy mini microphone to avoid sore throat when repetitively using high-tone voice in class.
