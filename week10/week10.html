SWOT ANALYSIS

STRENGTH

An active practicum teacher:

I never turned down any of the assignments they gave me before now. For this reason, I firmly believe that I have had the opportunity to develop my skills in order to become a top-tier teacher one day, even among my fellow practicum teachers.

WEAKNESS

Control of classes:

Large groupings of students make it difficult for me to keep up effective classroom management. However, I think the volume of my projection of speech is sufficient. The way the students behaved in large groups made me think of the way the majority of those teachers who work with them do the same thing. I'm going to reorganise their seating for my next class.

OPPORTUNITIES

In-class Exercise:

Because of their excellent level of English proficiency, I was able to carry out a lot of stuff during our instructional period because I was teaching three Inspirasi classes. More specifically, they are advanced learners who can pick up the material quickly, so it is preferable for them to learn while engaging in activities.

THREATS

Student - Teacher Interaction:

One could contend that I have to have the same experience. There is a specific set of students who always try to get my attention. Since it was our very first class together, I just believed that was the reason, since they wanted me to get to know and recognize them.
