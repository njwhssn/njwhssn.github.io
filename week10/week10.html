SWOT ANALYSIS

STRENGTH

An active practicum teacher:

Up until this point, I never rejected any tasks they assigned me to. Hence why, I best believe among other practicum teachers as well, I'm the one who got to enhance my skills to one day be a top-tier teacher.

WEAKNESS

Class control:

Since the students were grouped into large groups, I still struggle to maintain good classroom management. Nevertheless, I believe that my voice projection is loud enough. Because they were in big groups, the students' attitudes reminded me of what most of the teachers who instruct them also do. In my next class, I'm planning to rearrange their seating again.

OPPORTUNITIES

Class Activity:

Since I got 3 Inspirasi class to be taught, I managed to conduct a lot of activities during our lesson period due to their high level of proficiency in English. To explain more, they are advanced students and catch up the lesson even in a very short time, so it's better for them to learn while doing activities.

THREATS

Student - Teacher Interaction:

I could argue that I must experience it in the same way. A particular group of students consistently makes an effort to capture my interest. In any case, I simply assumed that was the reason, as they wanted me to know and recognise them, since it was our first class together.
