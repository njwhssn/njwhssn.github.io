SWOT ANALYSIS

STRENGTH

Teacher-student Interaction:

Throughout the first week of internship here, I noticed that students of my class have no problem in communicating with me. They are allowed to voice out their ideas or pov in formal or informal way or context (feel free to have a conversation with me too, not strictly about learning only). I also managed to solve a fight among my students.

I should have been more alert with my students doings and notice from the very start (the source of their fights). I also need to buy mini microphone to avoid sore throat when repetitively using high-tone voice in class.
