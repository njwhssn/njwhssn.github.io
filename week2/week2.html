OPPORTUNITIES

Flow of examination management:

Since I have been in this school for 2 weeks already, I finally got to learn this school's way of examination process. There are quite a lot of things that need to be aligned with, for example, Form 1,2 and 3 UASA schedules is on different days (not simultaneously). It is actually due to the UBBM (Ujian Bertutur Bahasa Melayu) for Form 5 students. The lower form will need to adhere to "Cuti Bergilir".

Overall of Week 2 is good and okay. However, at certain point, I believe that I must not feel awkward in advising all the students in SMK Taman Maluri, not only the classes that I taught, but all. On Tuesday, as I was walking along the corridor of PSV room, I noticed a couple of students were dating, not approaching or greeting the teacher at all. Actions are needed from me as well as the Discipline Unit of the school.
